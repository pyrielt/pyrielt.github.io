Getting started: Mario Royale Legacy !

Not endorsed by Nintendo. 2025 Spring Event promotional art by me.
This is a Super Mario browser fangame where you race against other players in classical levels from Super Mario Bros. worlds, other video game franchises, and even exclusive levels . Beyond a simple expansion of the original Mario Royale (2019) by InfernoPlus, it's become its own thing! I made a fifth of the current official maps in the game, all modes included. I also developed tiles, objects and other gameplay elements! (Several of these will be added to the game in a future major update.)
